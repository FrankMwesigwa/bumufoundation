Get Involved, Get Innovated

Bumu Foundation is a Non-Government Organization whose sole purpose is to build creative thinking communities in Africa, which will change the face of tomorrow. The foundation has been laid towards setting up the right habitat, bringing together like-minded individuals and their start-ups to encourage a vibrant ecosystem that stimulates innovation in tech and tech-enabled business, through interaction and ideation.

Invest

We partner with angle based investors and venture capitalists and other funding organizations to provide startup and early-stage entrepreneurs with seed funds and grants.

Build

We bring together technologies, talents and growth techniques to launch and grow ideas, either independently for stakeholders or in co-development with large corporations. We intend to build co-working partnerships between thriving industries and successful value-adding innovators.

Connect

We connect the best entrepreneurs with media, corporate investors while supporting the businesses from idea to market success. We also provide multipurpose co-working spaces for tech startups, social entrepreneurs and other similar SMEs to work together, share ideas & solutions, co-create.

Why do We Exist

The African continent is bursting with jewels and gems of ingenuity, hidden under a rough terrain. However, students in higher education or pending employment need a constructive environment to be productive. Independent contractors and entrepreneurs also need affordable work infrastructures and access to capacity-building resources. Moreover, traveler, returnees, and locals are in search of networking opportunities to facilitate meaningful partnerships with like-minded individuals. With such prospects, what better way than to unearth African talent through a communal space culture within a hub that facilitates collaboration, networking, and leisure?
